About

Raah Ek Mission is a charitable trust working towards Child Education and Welfare by providing after school programs, countering school dropouts, conducting donation drives, aiding welfare projects and organizing educational workshops.

Mission

Our Mission is focused on educating the under-privileged section, so that they can get this basic right, vital for not just a bright future but overall development of the individual. We firmly grip on this and are speeding up paving the way for the same by educating and providing scholarships.

Vision

Our vision is to light the lamp of basic education in every house and to support the individuals in their journey of life through free education, scholarships, stationary, etc. We aim to see that rising sun of India where, no one is deprived of basic education, or is suffering to get it.
